ANIMALS

DOG

The Dog is a one of the pet animal.The scientific name for the domestic dog is canis lupus familiaris.The dogs belongs to the Animalia Kingdom and Mammalia class.Dogs are loyal and affectionate companions, known for their strong bond with the humans.

TIGER

The Tiger is a member of the genus Panthera and the largest living cat species native to Asia.It belongs to the Mammalia class.The mass of the tiger is 90-310 Kg of Male tiger and 65-170 Kg of female tiger.The lifespan of each tiger is 10-15 years.To survive in the wild,tigers need large areas of habitat with sufficient water to drink,animals to eat,and and vegetative cover for hunting.The tiger is a very fast animal.It can reach a maximum speed of 50 to 65 kmph.

WOLF

The Wolf belongs to the Animalia kingdom and the Mammalia class.The mass of the Male adult Wolf is 30-80 Kg and the Female adult Wolf is 23-55 Kg.The Wolf also known as gray Wolf or grey wolf.The Wolf is most specialized for cooperative game hunting.In modern India,the Indian wolf is distributed across the states of Gujarat,Rajasthan,Haryana,Uttar Pradesh,Madhya Pradesh,Jarkhand,Maharashtra,Karnataka,Kerala and Andhra Pradesh.

DEER

The Deer belongs to the Animalia kingdom.Deers are specialized herbivores.It goes speed upto 60-80 Kmph.The scientific name of the Deer is Cervidae.It is a quite large animal with four legs that eats grass and leaves.It stands about 35 inches tall and weighs about 187 pounds(about 84 Kgs)
